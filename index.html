Features

Search

Find what you are looking for with our powerful and easy-to-use search engine.

Cart

Add items to your cart and check out with a few clicks. You can also save items for later or remove them if you change your mind.

Review

Read and write reviews for the products you buy or sell. You can also rate the sellers and buyers based on your experience.

About

I created this website as a portfolio project for Holberton School. I have always been interested in ecommerce and how it works. I wanted to learn how to build a website that can handle transactions, user accounts, and product listings. I also wanted to showcase my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This project took me about two months to complete, and I am very proud of the result.
